About Zahis International

Delivering Freshness Across Borders

Trusted global exporters of premium fruits and vegetables for over 20 years

For over 20 years, Zahis International has been a trusted leader in the global trade of fruits and vegetables. With a heritage rooted in quality, integrity, and transparency, we specialize in importing and exporting the finest produce—connecting farms, markets, and families worldwide.

Our farm-to-customer model ensures every fruit is handpicked at peak freshness, handled without artificial preservatives, and delivered while preserving its natural flavor, texture, and nutritional value.

At Zahis, we don’t just move produce—we cultivate partnerships. By importing from trusted international growers and re-exporting select items, we meet global demand with efficiency, reliability, and full compliance with international certifications.

Our Services

Organic Farm & Global Trade Services

Worldwide sourcing and export of premium fresh produce

Zahis International Food and Trading Company is recognized as a world leader in the supply of fresh produce. With export facilities across Australia, the United States, New Zealand, and Peru, we source and export fruits and vegetables worldwide with speed, safety, and consistency.

Our global logistics network ensures timely delivery, temperature-controlled handling, and compliance with international food safety standards.
