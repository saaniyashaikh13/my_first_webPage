Quality Control Process

Our rigorous quality control process ensures that every fruit meets the highest standards from farm to export. We focus on freshness, safety, and sustainability to deliver premium products.

Harvesting

Fruits are hand-picked at optimal ripeness by trained farmers.

Sorting & Grading

Automated and manual sorting to remove defects and grade by size and quality.

Testing

Laboratory tests for pesticides, ripeness, and nutritional value.

Packaging

Hygienic packaging with temperature-controlled storage.

Export

Final inspection before shipment to guarantee global standards.

Hygiene and Safety Standards

Our operations adhere to stringent hygiene and safety standards to ensure product integrity and consumer health.

  • Clean Facilities: Regular sanitization and pest control.
  • Employee Training: Hygiene protocols and safety gear for all staff.
  • Traceability: Full tracking from source to destination.
  • Compliance: Adherence to international food safety regulations.
  • Sustainable Practices: Eco-friendly methods to minimize environmental impact.
