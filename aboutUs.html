A trusted name in the international fruit export industry, delivering farm-fresh, premium-quality fruits to global markets with reliability, transparency, and excellence.

Who We Are

Zahis International is a globally trusted name in the import and export of premium fruits and vegetables, with over two decades of experience in delivering freshness across borders. Built on a foundation of quality, integrity, and long-term relationships, we serve as a reliable link between farmers, suppliers, distributors, and customers worldwide.

Our strength lies in our farm-to-customer model, designed to preserve the true essence of nature. Every product we handle is carefully sourced from fertile orchards and trusted farms, handpicked at peak ripeness, and managed with strict quality control—without the use of artificial preservatives. This ensures that the fruits and vegetables we deliver retain their natural flavor, texture, and nutritional value from the farm all the way to your market.

At Zahis International, we go beyond trading produce—we build partnerships. We collaborate closely with growers, exporters, logistics partners, and global buyers to create a transparent, efficient, and dependable supply chain. By importing high-quality produce from trusted international sources and re-exporting select items to meet global demand, we help businesses access fresh, safe, and premium agricultural products across markets. Our operations are fully aligned with major international certifications and global food safety standards, reflecting our commitment to quality assurance, sustainability, ethical sourcing, and environmental responsibility. Every shipment is handled with care, traceability, and compliance—so our partners can rely on consistency and confidence.

Whether sourcing from India’s fertile lands or delivering global specialties to international markets, Zahis International stands for purity, freshness, and trust—one shipment at a time. Our vision is to become a globally recognized fruit export brand known for trust, consistency, and long-term trade partnerships.
